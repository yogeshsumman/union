<p>Announcing <strong>U Combinator</strong>: a Union-focused incubator program in collaboration with <a href="https://crackedlabs.xyz/">Cracked Labs</a>. Taking place from Feb 18 to May 15, 50 teams will compete for 10 spots in the second cohort of the Union Fellowship, with workshops, prototyping, MVP feedback, and pitch practice along the way.</p>
<p>Here’s everything you need to know about U Combinator, including key dates, the benefits of becoming a Union Fellow, and why Union is partnering with Cracked Labs on this important project.</p>
<h2>Building the Next Wave of Union Ecosystem Projects</h2>
<p>The first <a href="https://x.com/union_build/status/1831363845648212418">Union Fellowship</a> was a huge success, with some of the first Union apps created and prototyped over the course of the fellowship. Over 200 builders applied to be part of the program, and <a href="https://x.com/union_build/status/1843589418898325719">11 builders from 5 countries</a> were selected for the first cohort.</p>
<p>The fellows leveled up through four weeks of workshops and collaborative building with the Union core team, as well as special sessions with ecosystem partners such as <a href="https://risczero.com/">Risc Zero</a> and <a href="https://agoric.com/">Agoric</a>. Then all of the fellows<a href="https://x.com/union_build/status/1848732531941884100"> joined the core team in Dubai</a> during Cosmoverse 2024 for a week of prototyping, brainstorming, and fun.</p>
<p>Since then, Union Fellows have been building incredible projects leveraging the Union network. The first wave of Union ecosystem projects are being built… which means it’s time to start on the next one.</p>
<h2>But Wait—Who are Cracked Labs?</h2>
<p><a href="https://crackedlabs.xyz/">Cracked Labs</a> is a Web3 and AI developer community born from one of the largest developer communities in the world, <a href="https://www.hackerearth.com/">HackerEarth</a>. As experts in developer events such as hackathons, incubators, accelerators, and hacking challenges, Cracked Labs are the ideal partners to make sure U Combinator is the best Web3 incubator in 2025.</p>
<p>Don’t believe it? Just watch. Or better yet, join in.</p>
<h2>U Combinator: An Incubator for the Union Ecosystem</h2>
<p>U Combinator is designed to expand on the success of the first Union Fellowship by bringing together even more cracked builders to learn, create, and compete.</p>
<p>50 teams will be accepted into the incubator, which will be a <strong>9-week program</strong> of technical workshops, building, pitching, and refining their projects. At the end of the program, the top 10 teams will be selected to become Union Fellows.</p>
<p>Here are the dates you need to know:</p>
<ul><li><p><strong>Feb 18</strong>: Registration is <strong>open</strong>. <a href="https://airtable.com/app8Pz2qQxkxm22C4/pag1TZSTwRMt5MboT/form">Apply here</a> when your team (and your pitch) is ready. Teams will be approved for the incubator throughout the registration period, so don’t wait too long to apply.</p></li><li><p><strong>Mar 15</strong>: Registration deadline.</p></li><li><p><strong>Mar 17 - Apr 13 (Weeks 1-4)</strong>: Technical workshops, office hours, and AMAs will provide teams with time to learn, refine their ideas, and get assistance from Union and Cracked Labs.</p></li><li><p><strong>Apr 14</strong>: Mid-incubator check-in. Teams will be asked to submit their working MVPs to receive detailed feedback from the Union and Cracked Labs teams.</p></li><li><p><strong>Apr 14 - May 11 (Weeks 5-7)</strong>: Polishing. Teams will be expected to refine their projects ahead of the project submission deadline, with more opportunities for feedback and learning from Union and Cracked Labs.</p></li><li><p><strong>May 12</strong>: Submissions due.</p></li><li><p><strong>May 15</strong>: Pitch day.</p></li></ul>
<p>The top 10 teams will be announced on May 23rd, and will become the second cohort of Union Fellows. That means white glove support from the Union core team, including technical, product design, marketing, bizdev, and fundraising support. It’s a huge asset to ambitious builders, so if that’s you, apply.</p>
<h2>Incubator Tracks</h2>
<p>U Combinator will be split into four tracks:</p>
<ol><li><p><strong>DeFi Social &amp; Creator Economy</strong></p><ul><li><p>Social media monetization platform</p></li><li><p>Crowdfunding platform for creators/projects</p></li><li><p>Revenue sharing and tokenization tools</p></li></ul></li><li><p><strong>Stablecoin Infrastructure</strong></p><ul><li><p>Stablecoin protocol development</p></li><li><p>Cross-chain liquidity and bridging</p></li><li><p>Integration with existing DeFi protocols</p></li></ul></li><li><p><strong>DeFAI &amp; Automation</strong></p><ul><li><p>AI agents on Union</p></li><li><p>Automated trading/investing strategies</p></li><li><p>AI-powered predictive analytics for DeFi yields</p></li></ul></li><li><p><strong>Market Infrastructure &amp; Incentives</strong></p><ul><li><p>Incentivized Action Market Protocol</p></li><li><p>Onchain behavior marketplace and bidding system</p></li><li><p>Market making infrastructure</p></li><li><p>Rewards/incentives optimization</p></li></ul></li></ol>
<p>Be clear about which track you’re aiming for in your application. There will be opportunities to learn and ask questions across all tracks during the incubator.</p>

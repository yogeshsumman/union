<p>Introducing <a href="https://dashboard.union.build/">Union Dashboard</a>, a custom platform aimed at unifying Union’s ecosystem and providing the community with ways to break the testnet, gain notoriety on mainnet, and be active on Union socials.</p>
<p>This dashboard, which is 100% created by cracked Union developers, launches with questing functionality. By completing missions and achievements, users will gain XP, rank up, and can earn rewards such as Discord roles, achievement cards, and potentially even a whitelist spot for the upcoming Wandering Whale Sharks mint.</p>
<ul><li><p>Go to <a href="https://dashboard.union.build/">dashboard.union.build</a>.</p></li><li><p>Connect your 𝕏/Twitter, Discord, and GitHub accounts.</p></li><li><p>Begin completing missions, unlocking achievements, and earning rewards for growing the Union ecosystem.</p></li></ul>
<p>Here’s everything you need to know about the dashboard at launch, and what to expect in the future.</p>
<h2>Missions, Achievements, &amp; Rewards</h2>
<img src="/content/assets/2eqmAiNedaFjlsdi2Whu5r/Union-Dashboard---Profile.png" alt="Screenshot of the user &quot;swgee_writes&quot; on the Union dashboard.">
<p>The dashboard launches with functionality designed to guide community interactions with Union. Missions, achievements, and rewards highlight what’s valuable to Union’s growth, and the leaderboard lets Unioneers compete to become top degens in the community.</p>
<p>Start by going to <a href="https://dashboard.union.build/">dashboard.union.build</a> and connecting your 𝕏/Twitter, Discord, and GitHub accounts. You only need one account to get started, but you’ll want to connect them all to complete social and community quests. Make sure a verified email address is associated with all accounts (ideally the same one for all accounts). For best results, connect all accounts in a single session.</p>
<p>Once you’re connected, a PFP will be pulled from your connected accounts <em>(NFT support coming soon)</em>, and an XP bar will be displayed. Be sure to <a href="https://dashboard.union.build/wallet">connect your wallets</a> too, for onchain quests.</p>
<p>Now it’s time to level up. Here's how:</p>
<h3>Missions</h3>
<img src="/content/assets/7L3UT1OTvjN78sDxSLOtXP/Screenshot-2025-01-20-at-23.56.05.png" alt="Screenshot of completed missions on the Union dashboard.">
<p>Missions are time-limited quests. Once the time has expired, incomplete missions will disappear and you’ll no longer be able to earn XP for them. Check back regularly for new missions.</p>
<h3>Achievements</h3>
<p>Achievements are split into categories: Onchain, Social, Communities, and Developer. Check each tab for achievements to complete, and see which ones you’ve already earned.</p>
<p>Included are retroactive achievements for the previous public testnet (Testnet 8) and contributing to the Trusted Setup Ceremony, as well as many that can be completed immediately. Achievements for Testnet 9 coming soon.</p>
<h3>Rewards</h3>
<p>Onchain and social rewards are available for Union superfans, such as…</p>
<ul><li><p><strong>Fanatic role.</strong> Join the <a href="https://discord.union.build/">Union Discord</a> before mainnet and get the special Fanatic role, plus check if you’re eligible for the rare OG role by having joined the Union Discord before Nov 15, 2024—AKA Year 1 of Union. Fanatics and OGs will play a key role in Union’s future.</p></li><li><p><strong>Wandering Whale Sharks whitelist spot.</strong> Certain achievements (or combinations of achievements) will award supporters with a whitelist spot for the Wandering Whale Sharks mint. Full details will be released soon, follow <a href="https://x.com/WhaleSharkNFTs">@WhaleSharkNFTs</a> for more.</p></li><li><p><strong>Achievement trading cards </strong><strong><em>(coming soon)</em></strong><strong>.</strong> Some achievements will award a random trading card for the new Union Trading Card Game*. Collect cards of different rarities, and build a deck of Union cards.</p></li></ul>
<p>*<em>Disclaimer: the Union TCG is fictional. There will be cards though, and they’re very cool.</em></p>

<p>Make sure to bookmark <a href="https://dashboard.union.build/">dashboard.union.build</a>. You’ll want to check back frequently for new missions, fresh achievements, more rewards, and new ways to engage with and grow the Union ecosystem.</p>

<p>We're excited to team up with <a href="https://www.avax.network/">Avalanche</a> to launch Project Ridge, which will bring trustless, super-efficient interoperability to Avalanche L1s.</p>
<p>This collaboration will create a trustless, secure <a href="https://www.ibcprotocol.dev/">IBC</a> connection to Avalanche L1s, enhancing liquidity and cross-chain efficiency. Union’s ZK technology and consensus verification will provide Avalanche L1s with access to all L1s, L2s, and appchains within Union’s network.</p>
<p>As part of the integration, Union will receive an <a href="https://www.avax.network/infrabuidl-program">InfraBUIDL() Grant</a> from the <a href="https://x.com/AvalancheFDN">Avalanche Foundation</a> to cover development and audit efforts.</p>
<h2>Why This Matters</h2>
<p>The Avalanche ecosystem is known for its commitment to decentralization, scalability, and performance. With Project Ridge, Union will integrate IBC Warp Messaging into Avalanche L1s using a highly secure, trustless light client architecture. This initiative will enable L1s to connect effortlessly for low-risk asset transfers and secure communication across any execution environment.</p>
<p>Here’s how Union aims to transform interoperability for Avalanche L1s:</p>
<ul><li><p><strong>Trustless Security</strong>: Unlike traditional bridging protocols that rely on multi-signature schemes and third-party trust, Union’s solution is based on trust-minimized light client architecture. This eliminates vulnerabilities such as double-spending and the risks associated with block reorganizations.</p></li><li><p><strong>Permissionless Integration</strong>: Any Avalanche L1 will have the option to join the IBC network autonomously, reinforcing decentralization and reducing the dependency on centralized bridge providers.</p></li><li><p><strong>Comprehensive Features</strong>: Union’s integration will unlock features like storage proofs, historic state queries, asset transfers, and account abstraction for Avalanche L1s, broadening the functional scope and flexibility of Avalanche’s ecosystem.</p></li></ul>
<p>Union Labs will develop two key components:</p>
<ul><li><p><strong>P-chain Light Client</strong>: A specification and implementation built in Rust, accompanied by comprehensive documentation and testing.</p></li><li><p><strong>ZK Circuit for P-chain Light Client</strong>: A zero-knowledge circuit complete with a verifier and robust testing to support the P-chain light client.</p></li></ul>
<p>This framework ensures a trustless, fast, and cost-efficient bridging solution by leveraging BLS signature aggregation, which reduces on-chain computation costs.</p>
<h2>The Benefits for Avalanche L1s</h2>
<p>Avalanche L1s will receive the following benefits from Project Ridge:</p>
<ul><li><p><strong>Speed and Efficiency</strong>: Union’s bridge solution ensures quick transaction generation and settlement, significantly reducing wait times and improving user experience.</p></li><li><p><strong>Security Without Compromise</strong>: By relying on consensus verification, Avalanche L1s will avoid common bridging vulnerabilities, creating a safe environment for asset and data transfers.</p></li><li><p><strong>Cost-effectiveness</strong>: ZK proofs and aggregated signature verification translates to lower operating costs compared to other bridging solutions.</p></li><li><p><strong>Connectivity to a Wider Ecosystem</strong>: Avalanche L1s will be able to engage with IBC-based and Union-connected projects.</p></li></ul>
<h2>Project Roadmap</h2>
<p>The development of Project Ridge will be kicked off in Q4 2024 and be available for testing in Q1 2025. The project will be divided into three phases:</p>
<ul><li><p><strong>Phase 1</strong>: P-Chain light client specification and implementation.</p></li><li><p><strong>Phase 2</strong>: Light client verification and packet transfer testing.</p></li><li><p><strong>Phase 3</strong>: Commercial deployment, rigorous audits, and co-marketing efforts.</p></li></ul>
